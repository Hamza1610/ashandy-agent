## Delivery Zones & Fees

### Within Ibadan (Same-Day or Next-Day Delivery)
Delivery fees are calculated based on distance from our shop in Iyaganku:

| Distance from Shop | Delivery Fee |
|-------------------|--------------|
| 0 - 8.7 km | ₦1,500 |
| 8.7 - 12.2 km | ₦2,000 |
| 12.2 - 13.1 km | ₦2,500 |
| 13.1+ km | ₦3,000 |

**Examples:**
- Bodija, Ring Road, Mokola → ₦1,500
- Akobo, Challenge, Ojoo → ₦2,000
- Alakia, Egbeda → ₦2,500

### Outside Ibadan (Nationwide)
- **Flat Rate:** ₦1,500
- **Delivery Time:** 2-3 business days
- We partner with reliable logistics companies for interstate deliveries

## Delivery Requirements

Before we can process your payment and delivery, we need:

1. **Full Name** - For package addressing
2. **Phone Number** - For rider contact
3. **Delivery Address** - Complete street address with landmarks

Without these details, we cannot generate your payment link.
