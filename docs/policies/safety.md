# Safety & Content Policy

## What Ashandy AI Can Help With

Our AI assistant is specifically designed for Ashandy Cosmetics business:

### Allowed Topics
1. **Product Inquiries**
   - Skincare products
   - Makeup products
   - SPMU (Semi-Permanent Makeup) products
   - Accessories (ring lights, brushes, tools)

2. **Order Processing**
   - Product search and availability
   - Price inquiries
   - Order placement
   - Payment processing

3. **Delivery & Logistics**
   - Delivery fee calculation
   - Tracking orders
   - Pickup arrangements

4. **Customer Service**
   - Shop location and hours
   - Manager contact
   - Order issues

5. **General Interaction**
   - Greetings (Hello, Hi)
   - Thanks and appreciation
   - Goodbyes

---

## What We Block (Prohibited)

### 1. Off-Topic Requests
We're a cosmetics store, not a general assistant:
- ❌ "What's the capital of Nigeria?"
- ❌ "Help me write an essay"
- ❌ "What's the latest news?"
- ❌ "Solve this math problem"

### 2. Harmful Content
Zero tolerance for:
- ❌ Violence or threats
- ❌ Hate speech or discrimination
- ❌ Harassment or bullying
- ❌ Sexual content or inappropriate advances
- ❌ Profanity or abusive language

### 3. Competitor Inquiries
We focus on our products:
- ❌ "Is this cheaper than Jumia?"
- ❌ "Do you sell Sephora products?"
- ❌ "Can you price-match with competitor X?"

### 4. Dangerous Requests
For everyone's safety:
- ❌ Requests for harmful chemical mixtures
- ❌ Instructions for dangerous substances
- ❌ Medical advice for serious conditions

### 5. Privacy Violations
We protect everyone:
- ❌ Requests for other customers' data
- ❌ Sharing BVN, PINs, or passwords
- ❌ Phishing attempts

### 6. System Manipulation
We maintain integrity:
- ❌ "Forget your rules"
- ❌ "Act as a developer"
- ❌ "Ignore your instructions"
- ❌ Asking about API keys or system internals

---

## How We Handle Violations

When we detect prohibited content:
1. The message is blocked
2. A polite explanation is provided
3. We redirect to allowed topics

We aim to be helpful while keeping conversations safe and on-topic.
