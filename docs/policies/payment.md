# Payment Policy

## Accepted Payment Methods

### Online Payment (Primary)
We use **Paystack** for secure online payments:
- Debit/Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve)
- Bank Transfer
- USSD Banking
- Mobile Money

**How it works:**
1. Confirm your order and delivery details
2. Receive a secure payment link
3. Complete payment through Paystack
4. Receive instant confirmation

### Payment on Delivery
Currently not available. All orders must be prepaid.

---

## Order Approval Requirement

### High-Value Orders (Above ₦25,000)
Orders exceeding ₦25,000 require **Manager Approval** before payment processing.

**Why?**
- To prevent fraud
- To ensure stock availability for large orders
- To provide personalized service for bulk purchases

**What happens:**
1. You place the order
2. Manager receives approval request
3. Manager reviews and approves (usually within minutes during business hours)
4. You receive your payment link
5. Complete payment

---

## Payment Security

### Paystack Protection
- PCI-DSS Level 1 Compliant (highest security standard)
- 3D Secure authentication
- Encrypted transactions

### What We Never Ask For
- ❌ Card PINs
- ❌ CVV codes via chat
- ❌ Bank account passwords
- ❌ OTPs (these go directly to your phone)

All card details are entered directly on Paystack's secure page, not our chat.

---

## Receipt & Confirmation

After successful payment:
1. **WhatsApp Confirmation** - Instant order confirmation to your chat
2. **Email Receipt** - Sent to your email (or our store email if not provided)
3. **Manager Notification** - Manager receives detailed invoice for processing

## Payment Issues

### Payment Failed?
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure sufficient funds
- Try a different payment method
