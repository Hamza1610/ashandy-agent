# Returns & Refunds Policy

## General Returns Policy

### Eligibility for Returns
Products may be returned if:
- Product is defective or damaged upon arrival
- Wrong product was delivered
- Product significantly differs from description

### Return Window
- Returns must be initiated within **48 hours** of delivery
- Contact us via WhatsApp immediately upon discovering an issue

### Non-Returnable Items
The following cannot be returned for hygiene reasons:
- Opened makeup products (lipsticks, mascaras, foundations)
- Opened skincare products
- Used brushes or applicators
- Pierced or punctured products

---

## How to Request a Return

1. **Contact Us** - Message us on WhatsApp within 48 hours
2. **Provide Details** - Order reference, issue description, photos if applicable
3. **Wait for Approval** - Manager will review and approve valid returns
4. **Return the Product** - Ship back or bring to our store
5. **Receive Resolution** - Refund or replacement as agreed

---

## Refund Policy

### Refund Methods
- **Original Payment Method** - Refund to your card/bank (3-7 business days)
- **Store Credit** - Instant credit for future purchases
- **Replacement** - Exchange for correct/undamaged product

### Refund Timeline
- Approval: Within 24 hours of return receipt
- Processing: 3-7 business days (bank-dependent)

---

## Damaged/Defective Products

If your product arrives damaged:
1. Do NOT use the product
2. Take photos of the damage (product + packaging)
3. Contact us immediately via WhatsApp
4. We'll arrange collection or replacement at no extra cost

---

## Wrong Product Delivered

If you received the wrong product:
1. Do NOT open the product
2. Contact us with your order details
3. We'll arrange pickup and send the correct item
4. No additional payment required

---

## Cancellations

### Before Payment
You can cancel anytime before completing payment.

### After Payment, Before Dispatch
- Full refund available
- Contact us immediately via WhatsApp

### After Dispatch
- Cannot cancel orders in transit
- Follow returns process if needed upon delivery
