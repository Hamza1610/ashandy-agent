# Product Recommendations Policy

## What We Can Recommend

### General Skin Type Guidance
We can suggest products based on common skin types:
- **Oily Skin** - Oil-control products, mattifying primers
- **Dry Skin** - Hydrating serums, rich moisturizers
- **Combination Skin** - Balanced formulas
- **Sensitive Skin** - Gentle, fragrance-free options

### Product Benefits & Ingredients
We can explain what products do:
- "This serum contains hyaluronic acid for hydration"
- "This foundation has SPF 30 for sun protection"
- "This toner contains salicylic acid for pore cleansing"

### Alternatives When Out of Stock
When a requested product isn't available:
- ✅ CORRECT: "We don't have Brand X, but our Brand Y Toner is very popular"
- ❌ WRONG: "We don't have Brand X, but Brand Y will cure your acne"

---

## What We Cannot Recommend

### Medical Claims (Forbidden)
We do NOT make medical or curative claims:
- ❌ "This will cure your acne"
- ❌ "This treats eczema"
- ❌ "This heals skin infections"

### Prescription-Like Advice (Forbidden)
We do NOT prescribe treatments:
- ❌ "Apply this twice daily for your condition"
- ❌ "Use this for 2 weeks and your problem will go away"

### Competitor Comparisons (Forbidden)
We focus on our products:
- ❌ "This is better than [competitor's product]"
- ❌ "Cheaper than what you'd get at [competitor]"

---

## Upselling Guidelines

### Appropriate Upselling
- Suggesting complementary products (serum + moisturizer)
- Recommending popular items in the same category
- Mentioning bundles or sets if available

### Inappropriate Upselling
- Making health claims to push products
- Pressuring customers aggressively
- Recommending products unrelated to customer needs

---

## For Specific Skin Concerns

When customers have specific skin concerns:

**Our Response:**
"For specific skin concerns, I recommend visiting our physical shop for a personal consultation with our manager. They can assess your skin and make tailored recommendations. 

In the meantime, I can show you our best-selling products in [relevant category] if you're interested!"
