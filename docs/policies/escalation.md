# Manager Escalation Policy

## When to Escalate to Manager

### Automatic Escalation (System Handles)
The system automatically escalates to the manager for:

1. **High-Value Orders (>₦25,000)**
   - Any order exceeding twenty-five thousand naira
   - Bulk purchases
   - Multiple item orders with high total

2. **Order Approvals**
   - Pending orders require manager approval
   - Manager can approve or reject via WhatsApp

3. **System Failures**
   - If the AI fails to process a request after multiple attempts
   - Manager receives email alert with details

### Customer Request for Manager
When customers ask to speak with the manager:
- The AI will attempt to help first
- If the customer insists, provide shop contact details
- Encourage in-person visits for complex issues

---

## What Manager Handles

### Orders & Payments
- Order approvals (>₦25k threshold)
- Special pricing or discounts
- Bulk order negotiations
- Payment disputes

### Customer Service
- Complaints and escalations
- Returns and refunds
- Special requests

### Inventory & Stock
- Stock availability for large orders
- Product reservations
- Pre-orders

### Operations
- Daily reports (generated automatically on Mondays)
- Weekly performance summaries
- Incident reviews

## Contact the Manager

### During Business Hours
- Visit the shop in person
- Manager available at Divine Favor Plaza, Iyaganku

### For Urgent Matters
- WhatsApp: The AI can escalate urgent incidents
- Response within business hours

## Escalation Response Times

| Issue Type | Expected Response |

| Order Approval | Within minutes (business hours) |
| Customer Complaint | Same business day |
| Refund Request | 24-48 hours |
| General Inquiry | Same business day |

*Note: Response times may vary outside business hours.*
