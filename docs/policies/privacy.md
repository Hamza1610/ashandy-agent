# Privacy Policy

## Why We Collect Customer Information

### Phone Number (Required)
We need your phone number so that:
- Our delivery rider can contact you when they arrive
- We can send you order updates and delivery notifications via SMS
- We can reach you if there are any issues with your order

### Delivery Address (Required for Delivery Orders)
Your address is needed to:
- Ensure your order arrives safely at your exact doorstep
- Calculate accurate delivery fees based on your location
- Provide the rider with clear directions

### Full Name (Required)
We ask for your name so that:
- We can address your package correctly
- Our delivery rider knows who to hand the package to
- We can personalize your receipt and communications

### Email (Optional)
Your email is used to:
- Send you a digital receipt after payment
- Notify you of order confirmations
- Share special promotions (with your consent)

**Note:** If you don't provide an email, we use our store email (ashandyawelewa@gmail.com) for Paystack transactions.

---

## Data Protection & Privacy Assurance

### What We Promise
- ✅ We only use your information to process and deliver your order
- ✅ We never share your data with third parties for marketing
- ✅ Your payment details are handled securely by Paystack (PCI-DSS compliant)
- ✅ We comply with Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR)

### Data Retention
- Order records are kept for accounting and customer service purposes
- You can request deletion of your data by contacting us through WhatsApp

### Security Measures
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Access to customer data is limited to authorized personnel only
- We do not store card details - Paystack handles all payment processing

---

## Prohibited Information (We Will Never Ask For)
- ❌ BVN (Bank Verification Number)
- ❌ ATM PINs or passwords
- ❌ Full card numbers (Paystack handles this securely)
- ❌ Account passwords

If anyone claiming to be from Ashandy asks for these, it's a scam. Report immediately.
